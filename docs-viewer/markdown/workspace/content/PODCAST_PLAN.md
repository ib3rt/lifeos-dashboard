- "This week I automated X"
- "I built 3 websites in 24 hours"
- "My AI agent army deployment"
- Behind-the-scenes of system building

Review and demonstrate new AI tools:
- "I tested 5 coding agents so you don't have to"
- "This $20 tool replaced my $500/month stack"
- "Local vs Cloud: The Real Numbers"

### 3. System Showcases (20%)
Listeners submit their workflows, we optimize:
- "Reddit user's Notion setup ‚Üí Automated"
- "From chaos to PARA: A transformation"

### 4. Industry Commentary (10%)
AI news that actually matters:
- "GPT-5: Hype vs Reality"
- "The Death of SaaS? (Probably Not)"
