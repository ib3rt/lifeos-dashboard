## Quick Reference

### Recommended Structures by Scenario

| Scenario | Recommended Structure | Primary Jurisdiction |
|----------|----------------------|---------------------|
| Solo operator, <$100K revenue | Single-Member LLC | Wyoming |
| Growing business, $100K-$300K | LLC (S-Corp election) OR Series LLC | Wyoming / Nevada |
| Established business, >$300K | C-Corp with IP Subsidiary | Delaware |
| Real estate portfolio | LLC (Parent) + LLC per property | Wyoming / South Dakota |
| Multiple business lines | Series LLC OR Multi-entity Holding | Nevada / Delaware |
| International operations | **CONSULT SPECIALIZED COUNSEL** | Varies |

### Cost Summary

| Structure Type | Initial Cost | Annual Cost |
|----------------|--------------|-------------|
| Single LLC | $100-500 | $50-800 |
| Multi-entity Holding (3 subs) | $4,250-9,400 | $5,000-11,300 |
| Series LLC | $3,350-6,550 | $2,850-6,550 |
| Offshore (Cayman) | $12,000-30,000 | $10,000-27,000 |

---

## Key Takeaways

1. **Wyoming** offers the strongest domestic asset protection with lowest costs
2. **S-Corp election** can save significant self-employment taxes for profitable businesses
3. **Series LLCs** offer cost savings but have legal uncertainty
4. **Offshore structures** require specialized counsel and extensive compliance
5. **Always separate IP assets** from operating liabilities
6. **Consult professionals** before making any formation decisions
