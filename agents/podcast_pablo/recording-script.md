## PART 1: PRE-RECORDING WARM-UP (2-3 minutes)

### Breathing Exercise (30 seconds)
Take a deep breath in through your nose for 4 counts... hold for 4... and release slowly through your mouth. Let's do this three times. Feel your shoulders relax. Your voice works best when your body is calm and your breath is supported from your diaphragm, not your throat.

### Vocal Warm-Up: The Hum (30 seconds)
Start with a gentle hum. Mmmmmmm. Feel the vibration in your face, your chest. Move up and down your range naturally. Don't force it‚Äîjust let your voice wake up. Humming engages your vocal cords without strain.

### Articulation Exercise: Tongue Twisters (1 minute)
Let's get those articulators moving clearly:

"Unique New York, Unique New York. You know you need unique New York."

"Red lorry, yellow lorry. Red lorry, yellow lorry."

"She sells seashells by the seashore. The shells she sells are surely seashells."

"Peter Piper picked a peck of pickled peppers."

Say these at a natural conversational pace. Don't rush. Clarity matters more than speed.

### Range and Expression Practice (1 minute)
Now let's practice some vocal variety. Read this sentence three different ways:

**Neutral:** "The system is now operational."

**Excited:** "The system is now operational!"

**Thoughtful/Questioning:** "The system is... now operational?"

This helps capture your natural range of expression for the AI to learn.

## PART 4: POST-RECORDING CHECKLIST

### Immediate Actions:

1. **Listen Back** - Play the recording through headphones. Check for:
   - Background noise (AC, traffic, computer fans)
   - Mouth clicks or pops
   - Inconsistent volume levels
   - Any interruptions or mistakes

2. **Export Settings** - Save as:
   - Format: WAV or MP3 (WAV preferred for quality)
   - Sample Rate: 44.1 kHz or 48 kHz
   - Bit Depth: 16-bit or 24-bit
   - No compression if possible

3. **File Naming** - Use clear naming:
   - `b3rt-voice-clone-[date].wav`
   - Example: `b3rt-voice-clone-2026-02-03.wav`

### Before Uploading to ElevenLabs:

4. **Trim Silence** - Remove long pauses at beginning and end
5. **Normalize Audio** - Ensure consistent volume throughout
6. **Optional: Light Noise Reduction** - Only if there's noticeable background hum
   - Don't over-process! Natural is better than perfect

### Upload Process:

7. Go to https://elevenlabs.io/voice-lab
8. Click "Add Generative or Cloned Voice"
9. Select "Instant Voice Cloning"
10. Upload your audio file
11. Name it: `b3rt-podcast-host`
12. Wait for processing (1-5 minutes)
