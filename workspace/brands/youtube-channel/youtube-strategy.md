# Faceless YouTube Channel Plan
*Content Empire Strategy*

---

## Channel Concept: **"Systematic"**

**Niche:** Productivity, automation, and life optimization for technical professionals
**Angle:** Show systems and workflows, not faces. Screen recordings, animations, B-roll.
**Target:** 25-45 year old developers, engineers, tech-savvy professionals

---

## Content Pillars

| Pillar | % | Examples |
|--------|---|----------|
| **Tool Reviews** | 30% | "I Automated My Entire Life with OpenClaw" |
| **System Builds** | 25% | "Building a $10K/month Airbnb Business" |
| **Workflow Breakdowns** | 25% | "My PARA Productivity System Explained" |
| **Case Studies** | 15% | "From 0 to 100K: Channel Growth Breakdown" |
| **Trend Commentary** | 5% | "Why AI Agents Will Replace VAs" |

---

## Faceless Formats

1. **Screen Recordings** (primary)
   - Tool demonstrations
   - System walkthroughs
   - Code/terminal sessions

2. **Motion Graphics**
   - Animated diagrams
   - Data visualizations
   - Process flows

3. **B-Roll + Voiceover**
   - Stock footage of workspaces
   - Hands typing, coffee, planners
   - Drone shots of cities

4. **AI-Generated Visuals**
   - Midjourney/Stable Diffusion art
   - Consistent visual style
   - Character-free imagery

## Brand Identity

**Visual Style:**
- Dark mode interface recordings
- Teal/blue accent colors (#0D9488, #1E40AF)
- Clean, minimal overlays
- Consistent lower-third graphics

**Voice/Tone:**
- Direct, no fluff
- Technical but accessible
- "I've tested this" authority
- Action-oriented

**Music:**
- Lo-fi hip hop (productivity vibe)
- No vocals
- Consistent across videos
