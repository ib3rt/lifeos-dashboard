# BE Repaired — Brand Guidelines
*Handyman & Repair Services*

---

## Brand Essence

### Mission Statement
**"Fix it right. Fix it once."**

We solve home repair problems permanently, combining old-school craftsmanship with modern reliability. No band-aids, no callbacks—just done.

### Tagline Options
1. **"Your Home, Handled."** (recommended)
2. "Built to Last Repairs"
3. "The Fix You Can Forget"

---

## Target Audience

### Primary: Homeowners
- **Demographics:** 35-65 years old, established in homes 3+ years
- **Psychographics:** Values quality over cheap fixes, tired of handyman roulette, wants someone who shows up
- **Pain Points:** No-shows, shoddy work, mysterious pricing, projects that drag on
- **Aspirations:** Well-maintained home, pride of ownership, peace of mind

### Secondary: Property Managers
- Residential property managers
- Need reliable vendor for tenant repairs
- Value clear communication and documentation

### Tertiary: Airbnb/STR Hosts
- Cross-sell from Sparkling Solutions
- Quick repairs between guests
- Emergency fixes (leaks, AC, etc.)

---

## Brand Personality

| Trait | Expression |
|-------|------------|
| **Skilled** | Master tradesman, not a jack-of-all-trades |
| **Reliable** | Shows up when promised, finishes on time |
| **Honest** | Transparent pricing, no upsell, admits when outside scope |
| **Craftsman** | Takes pride in work, cleans up after |
| **Straightforward** | No jargon, clear communication |

**Voice & Tone:**
- Direct and clear ("Here's what I found" not "It appears that...")
- Confident but not arrogant
- Educational (explains the why, not just the what)
- Respectful of customer's home and time

## Visual Identity

### Color Palette

| Role | Color | Hex | Usage |
|------|-------|-----|-------|
| **Primary** | Craftsman Blue | `#1E40AF` | Logo, headers, trust |
| **Secondary** | Steel Gray | `#475569` | Body text, subtle |
| **Accent** | Tool Orange | `#EA580C` | CTAs, alerts, important |
| **Neutral** | Concrete | `#94A3B8` | Backgrounds, dividers |
| **Dark** | Workboot Black | `#0F172A` | Footer, contrast |
| **Light** | Shop White | `#F8FAFC` | Clean backgrounds |

**Color Psychology:**
- Blue = Trust, reliability, professional
- Orange = Action, tools, caution
- Gray = Industrial, practical, grounded
- Black = Solid, enduring, no-nonsense

### Typography

| Role | Font | Weight | Usage |
|------|------|--------|-------|
| **Headlines** | Bebas Neue | 400 | Bold, strong statements |
| **Body** | Roboto | 400-500 | Clean, readable, modern |
| **Accent** | Roboto Mono | 400 | Prices, specs, lists |

**Google Fonts Import:**
```css
@import url('https://fonts.googleapis.com/css2?family=Bebas+Neue&family=Roboto:wght@400;500;700&family=Roboto+Mono&display=swap');
```

### Logo Concept

**Design:**
- Wordmark "BE" in Bebas Neue (large, bold)
- "Repaired" in Roboto (smaller, beneath)
- Icon: Stylized wrench integrated with "E" (or standalone)
- The "BE" implies both "Brandon Edwards" (personal) and "Being Repaired" (state)

**Variations:**
1. **Primary:** BE (large) + Repaired + wrench icon
2. **Compact:** BE + wrench (for tight spaces)
3. **Horizontal:** Wrench left, full wordmark right
4. **One-color:** For invoices, stamps, single-color printing

## Services Framework

### Tier 1: Quick Fixes (1-2 hours)
- Fixture installation/replacement
- Door/hardware adjustments
- Drywall patch & paint (small)
- Caulking
- Light electrical (switches, outlets)
- Light plumbing (faucets, toilets)

### Tier 2: Half-Day Projects (3-5 hours)
- Door installation
- Fan/light installation
- TV mounting + cable management
- Tile repair (small areas)
- Cabinet hardware replacement
- Minor carpentry

### Tier 3: Full-Day Projects (6-8 hours)
- Flooring repair (sections)
- Deck/patio repairs
- Bathroom refresh (non-plumbing)
- Kitchen backsplash
- Window/door trim

### Tier 4: Multi-Day/Referral
- Full bathroom remodel (refer to specialist)
- Kitchen remodel (refer to specialist)
- Major electrical (refer to licensed electrician)
- Major plumbing (refer to licensed plumber)

**Positioning:** "We handle 90% of home repairs. For the other 10%, we'll refer you to the right specialist—we know who's good."

## Website Architecture

### Pages

1. **Homepage**
   - Hero: "Your Home, Handled."
   - Trust badges (Licensed, Bonded, 20+ Years)
   - Service categories (Quick Fixes → Full Projects)
   - Before/after gallery
   - Testimonials with photos
   - CTA: Get Free Estimate

2. **Services**
   - Grid of service categories with icons
   - Typical jobs list per category
   - Pricing philosophy (upfront quotes)
   - What's included / not included

3. **About**
   - Craftsman's story (journey to this work)
   - Philosophy (fix it right, fix it once)
   - Service area map
   - Credentials & insurance

4. **Projects/Portfolio**
   - Before/after gallery
   - Project details (time, scope)
   - Client testimonials

5. **FAQ**
   - Do you charge hourly or by job?
   - What if you can't fix it?
   - Emergency services?
   - Warranty policy

6. **Contact/Estimate**
   - Form: Job type, description, photos
   - Phone: Call/text
   - Typical response time
   - Service area confirmation

## Competitive Differentiation

| Competitor | Their Angle | Our Counter |
|------------|-------------|-------------|
| Cheap handyman apps | Fast, cheap | Quality, reliable, shows up |
| Big contractors | Full remodels | Repairs and small jobs (often refused by big guys) |

| Solo Craigslist | Cheap | Licensed, insured, professional |

**Unique Selling Proposition:**
"The repairman who treats your home like his own—shows up on time, fixes it right, and leaves it cleaner than he found it."

## Cross-Promotion with Sparkling Solutions

**Natural Synergy:**
- Cleaning finds issues → Repair fixes them
- Hosts need both services
- Shared credibility (if one is great, other must be)

**Tactical Integration:**
- Sparkling Solutions cleaners report issues to BE Repaired
- Bundle discount: 10% off when booking both
- Shared branding elements (colors differ, but quality promise aligns)
- Co-marketing: "Your Complete Airbnb Solution"
