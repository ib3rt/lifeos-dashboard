# Sparkling Solutions — Brand Guidelines
*Airbnb Cleaning Service*

---

## Brand Essence

### Mission Statement
**"Exceptional cleanliness, effortless hosting."**

We transform rental properties into 5-star experiences, freeing hosts to focus on hospitality while we perfect presentation.

### Tagline Options
1. **"Where Clean Meets Keen"** (recommended)
2. "Spotless. Seamless."
3. "Hosts Trust. Guests Love."

---

## Target Audience

### Primary: Airbnb Hosts
- **Demographics:** 28-55 years old, tech-savvy, side hustlers to portfolio managers
- **Psychographics:** Value time over money, care deeply about reviews, want hassle-free operations
- **Pain Points:** Last-minute turnovers, inconsistent quality, finding reliable cleaners
- **Aspirations:** Superhost status, passive income, property expansion

### Secondary: Property Managers
- Small-to-medium portfolio operators (5-50 properties)
- Need scalable, consistent service
- Value communication and reliability

---

## Brand Personality

| Trait | Expression |
|-------|------------|
| **Professional** | On-time, uniformed, systematic |
| **Detail-Obsessed** | 100-point checklist, photo documentation |
| **Tech-Forward** | App-based scheduling, automated updates |
| **Trustworthy** | Bonded, insured, vetted teams |
| **Energetic** | Fast turnarounds, can-do attitude |

**Voice & Tone:**
- Confident but humble (we're the experts, but you're the boss)
- Action-oriented ("We'll handle it" not "We can try")
- Warm but professional (friendly, not casual)

## Visual Identity

### Color Palette

| Role | Color | Hex | Usage |
|------|-------|-----|-------|
| **Primary** | Fresh Teal | `#0D9488` | Logo, headers, CTAs |
| **Secondary** | Clean White | `#FFFFFF` | Backgrounds, space |
| **Accent** | Sunny Gold | `#F59E0B` | Highlights, stars, ratings |
| **Neutral** | Slate Gray | `#64748B` | Body text, subtle elements |
| **Dark** | Deep Navy | `#1E293B` | Footer, contrast |

**Color Psychology:**
- Teal = Clean, trustworthy, refreshing
- Gold = Quality, 5-star excellence
- White = Purity, simplicity
- Navy = Professionalism, stability

### Typography

| Role | Font | Weight | Usage |
|------|------|--------|-------|
| **Headlines** | Poppins | 600-700 | Brand name, major headers |
| **Body** | Inter | 400-500 | Paragraphs, descriptions |
| **Accent** | Playfair Display | 400 | Quotes, testimonials |

**Google Fonts Import:**
```css
@import url('https://fonts.googleapis.com/css2?family=Inter:wght@400;500;600&family=Playfair+Display&family=Poppins:wght@600;700&display=swap');
```

### Logo Concept

**Design:**
- Wordmark "Sparkling" in Poppins Bold, "Solutions" in Poppins Regular
- Icon: Stylized sparkle/star with 4 points (representing thoroughness)
- The sparkle incorporates a subtle "S" negative space

**Variations:**
1. **Primary:** Icon left + wordmark (horizontal)
2. **Stacked:** Icon above wordmark (square formats)
3. **Icon Only:** For favicon, app icon
4. **White:** For dark backgrounds

## Photography Style

**Hero Images:**
- Bright, airy interiors with natural light
- Wide-angle shots showing space, not just cleanliness
- Diverse properties (urban loft, suburban home, beach cottage)
- Before/after transitions (subtle, not gross)

**Detail Shots:**
- Macro of gleaming surfaces
- Fluffy towels artfully arranged
- Fresh flowers, coffee station setups
- Happy guests entering (stock, authentic)

**Team Photos:**
- Professional uniforms (teal polo, navy pants)
- Friendly but focused expressions
- In-action shots (vacuuming, staging, inspecting)
