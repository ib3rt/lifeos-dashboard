### Strategy 1: Privacy by Design (PbD)

**The 7 Foundational Principles:**

1. **Proactive not Reactive**: Anticipate privacy issues before they arise
2. **Privacy as Default**: Maximum privacy settings out of the box
3. **Privacy Embedded**: Build privacy into architecture, not bolt it on
4. **Full Functionality**: Privacy AND functionality, not privacy OR functionality
5. **End-to-End Protection**: Lifecycle security from collection to destruction
6. **Visibility & Transparency**: Open about data practices
7. **Respect for User Privacy**: User-centric approach
